PLAY OTHELLO ONLINE
INSTRUCTIONS
1. This is a two player game.
2. Players should click on one of the dotted border circles during their move to add a disc.
3. The player with the most discs on the board at the end of the game wins.
4. The game ends either when all the squares are occupied or when neither of the player can move.
5. At the end of the game click on any square of the board to know result.
6. To know more about the game click here.
